Our Lessons

Discover the benefits of our personalized one-on-one lessons designed to meet your individual goals and objectives.

Easy Booking System

Just look for a teacher, find a slot, and you're ready to go!

Review Your Lessons

Post-lesson you'll have a record giving you advice on how you can improve, and review the vocabulary and English expressions you learn in the lesson.

Customized to Meet Individual Goals

Teachers will customize lessons for your needs. Whether you want to improve conversation skills, communication, or practice specific situations and scenarios, we'll do our best to meet your needs.

Lessons at a Pace That Suits Everyone

Proceed at your own pace. You can focus on what you want to learn: our teachers are always happy to help!

Future Possibilities

ChatBot

A chatbot will be set up specifically to help students outside of lesson times.

Flashcard System

Lesson records will link with Anki to allow the 'new_words' and 'new_phrases' to be automatically added with a translation for practice at home.

Multi-Language Support

Add support for other languages: French, Spanish, and Italian are regularly taught in Japan.

Mobile App

Using Electron, develop a mobile app version of the platform for easier access on mobile devices.

Video Lesson Integration

Integrate with video conferencing tools like Zoom or Google Meet to facilitate online lessons.

Pronunciation Helper

Practice words you find difficult to pronounce with an AI helper that will help you master speaking clearly!
